Introduction

Welcome to "5 Star German", where tradition meets modern flair! Nestled in the heart of Germany, our restaurant brings you authentic German cuisine with a touch of contemporary elegance. From hearty sausages and pretzels to refined takes on schnitzel and sauerbraten, we celebrate the rich flavors rooted in centuries of German culinary history. Inspired by the diversity of Germany’s regional dishes, *5 Star German* promises a dining experience that reflects the heart of Bavarian feasts and the soul of Berlin’s modern kitchens. Come taste the pride of Germany, one star at a time!

Menu

1. Sauerbraten (Braised Beef Roast) – €22.50 A classic German pot roast marinated in vinegar, spices, and herbs, slowly braised to perfection. Served with red cabbage and potato dumplings for a hearty, flavorful meal.

2. Wiener Schnitzel (Breaded Veal Cutlet) – €18.90 A golden, crispy breaded veal cutlet, served with a side of buttery potatoes and fresh lemon wedges. A beloved German specialty with Austrian roots.

3. Bratwurst with Sauerkraut – €14.50 Grilled pork sausages served with tangy sauerkraut and a side of mustard. This dish celebrates the traditional flavors of Bavarian street food.

4. Käsespätzle (Cheesy Egg Noodles) – €12.00 Germany’s take on mac and cheese, these soft egg noodles are baked with a blend of German cheeses, topped with caramelized onions, and served with a fresh side salad.

5. Rinderroulade (Beef Rolls) – €19.80 Thin slices of beef rolled with pickles, mustard, and onions, slow-cooked to tender perfection. Served with mashed potatoes and a rich gravy.

Chef Bio

Meet Chef Markus Keller, the culinary genius behind *5 Star German*. Born and raised in the Bavarian countryside, Markus grew up surrounded by the rich aromas of traditional German cooking. Trained in some of Germany’s finest kitchens, he blends his deep respect for age-old recipes with a creative modern twist. With over 15 years of experience, Chef Keller has mastered the art of transforming classic dishes into gourmet experiences, earning a reputation for his innovation and passion for fresh, local ingredients. His goal at *5 Star German*? To bring the authentic taste of Germany to every plate, one unforgettable dish at a time!
